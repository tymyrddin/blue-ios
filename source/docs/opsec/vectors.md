# Attack vector awareness

Attack vectors are methods or channels an attacker uses to gain access to a system or network, with the goal of delivering a payload or exploit. These are the most common mobile attack vectors, and what you can do to defend against them.

* [Phishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/phishing)
* [Smishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/smishing)
* [Vishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/vishing)
* [SIM swapping](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/swapping)
* [MFA bypass attacks](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/mfa)
* [OTP bots](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/otp-bots)
* [Drive-by downloads](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/drive-by)
