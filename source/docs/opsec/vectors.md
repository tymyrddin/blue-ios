# Attack vector awareness

Attack vectors are methods or channels an attacker uses to gain access to a system or network, with the goal of delivering a payload or exploit. These are the most common mobile attack vectors, and what you can do to defend against them.

* [Phishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/phishing.md)
* [Smishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/smishing.md)
* [Vishing](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/vishing.md)
* [SIM swapping](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/swapping.md)
* [OTP bots](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/otp-bots.md)
* [Drive-by downloads](https://pap.tymyrddin.dev/docs/notes/drive-by.md)
